✦  About ViralizeAI

One essay.
Three viral formats.

ViralizeAI uses Google's Gemini AI to read your long-form content and intelligently repurpose it into the format that works best for each platform — saving you hours of manual editing.

What it creates

𝕏

Twitter / X Thread

A punchy 6–10 tweet thread with a hook, flow, and a CTA — each tweet under 280 characters.

💼

LinkedIn Post

A professional yet engaging post with a scroll-stopping opener, insights, and hashtags.

🎬

Reel / Shorts Script

A ~30-second spoken script with visual cues, designed for Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts.

How to use it

1

Paste your content

Copy any essay, blog post, article, or report — at least a few sentences long — and paste it into the input box.

2

Choose a format

Select one of the three output formats: Twitter/X Thread, LinkedIn Post, or Reel/Shorts Script.

3

Hit Generate

The AI will craft your content in seconds. The result appears in a card below.

4

Try other formats

Your earlier results stay saved. Pick another format and generate — swipe between all your cards to compare.

5

Copy and post

Hit the Copy button on any card to copy the content to your clipboard, then paste it directly on your platform.

💡 Tips for best results

  • Use detailed, structured content — the more context, the sharper the output.
  • Academic essays, blog posts, news reports, and research summaries all work great.
  • You can regenerate any format as many times as you like — each run produces a unique result.
  • For Reels, read the script aloud after generating to check your pacing.
